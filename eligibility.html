💡 How to Increase Your Loan Eligibility

💰 Add a co-applicant
Adding a working spouse or parent as co-applicant combines both incomes — can double your eligibility. Most banks allow this.
📈 Improve CIBIL score first
Moving from 650 to 750 can increase your eligible loan by 15–20% as banks offer lower rates to better scores.
💳 Close existing loans first
Each ₹5,000 in existing EMI reduces eligibility by ₹3–5 lakh. Closing small loans before applying helps significantly.
📄 Include all income sources
Rental income, part-time income, and investment returns can be included. Show bank statements for all income sources.
⏱️ Choose longer tenure
A 30-year home loan has lower EMI than 15-year, so you qualify for a larger amount. You can always prepay later.
🏦 Try NBFCs if banks reject
NBFCs like Bajaj Finance, HDFC Ltd, and Tata Capital have more flexible income criteria than traditional banks.
