Instant Loans for Gig Workers

Ola, Uber, Swiggy, Zomato, Dunzo, Urban Company, freelancers — banks reject you because you have no salary slip. Here's how to get approved anyway.

❌ Why banks reject gig workers
❌No salary slip or Form 16
❌Irregular monthly income
❌No fixed employer
❌Thin or no credit history

✅ Best apps that accept gig workers

KreditBee
✅ Accepts gig income
Accepts bank statement showing regular credits. Ola/Uber/Swiggy payout statements accepted. Min ₹10,000/month average over 3 months.
MoneyView
✅ Bank statement-based
Uses 3-month bank statement for income proof. Min ₹13,500 average monthly credit. Works well for delivery partners and freelancers.
CASHe
✅ Social score-based
Uses a proprietary social loan score. Good for those with no credit history but regular digital/UPI transactions. Min ₹12,000/month.
PaySense
✅ Self-employed friendly
Specifically designed for self-employed. Min ₹12,000/month. Bank statement of 6 months required. Accepts freelancer income.
💡 5 Tips to Get Approved as a Gig Worker
1
Keep all gig earnings in one bank account — consistent credits improve approval chances dramatically
2
Maintain a 6-month bank statement with regular credits above ₹12,000/month before applying
3
Get a secured credit card (₹5,000 FD-backed) and use it to build CIBIL score — helps get lower rates
4
Apply to KreditBee or MoneyView first — they have the most flexible gig income verification
5
Keep UPI transaction history active — many apps now use UPI data as an income proof signal

Safety Tips — Avoid Loan App Scams

🚫 Red flags — avoid these apps immediately
❌Not listed on RBI's NBFC list
❌Asks for contacts or gallery access
❌Promises loan without any verification
❌Charges fee before disbursing loan
❌No physical address or grievance officer
❌Communicates only via WhatsApp
✅ How to verify a loan app is genuine
1
Check RBI's approved NBFC list at rbi.org.in → NBFC list → search app name
2
App must be on Google Play Store with 1 lakh+ downloads and 3.5+ star rating
3
Read the loan agreement fully before accepting — check all fees and penalty clauses
4
Never share OTP or net banking password with anyone claiming to be from the app
5
Report fake loan apps at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
