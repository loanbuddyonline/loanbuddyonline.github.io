About LoanBuddy

We built the tool we wished existed when we were applying for our first loan — clear, honest, and completely free.

🎯 Our Mission

India has over 50 crore loan seekers — salaried employees, gig workers, students, small business owners — who get rejected, overcharged, or misled by the loan system every day. Banks use complex jargon. NBFC apps hide fees in fine print. Agents earn commissions for recommending the wrong product.

LoanBuddy exists to fix this. We give every Indian borrower — regardless of income level — the same quality of information that a financial advisor charges ₹5,000 to provide. Free. With real calculators that use the actual bank formulas.

🛠️ What LoanBuddy Does

📊 Real Calculators
Our EMI and eligibility calculators use the actual reducing balance formula and FOIR method — the same math banks use. Not approximations.
🏦 Honest Comparisons
We compare loan products across banks and NBFCs without favoring any lender. No paid placements. No "sponsored" results.
⚡ Gig Worker Focus
Most finance sites ignore India's 10 crore+ gig workers. We specifically cover which apps and lenders accept gig income, bank statements, and no salary slip.
📚 Plain Language
No banking jargon. We explain FOIR, CIBIL, EMI, prepayment — everything — in simple terms anyone can understand.

⚠️ What We Are Not

❌ We are not a lender. We do not give loans directly.
❌ We are not a DSA or loan agent. We earn no commission from lenders (currently).
❌ We do not sell your data. We do not share your contact with banks or third parties.
❌ We are not SEBI/RBI registered. Nothing on this site is financial advice. Always verify rates directly with the lender before applying.

💡 Our Values

🔍
Transparency
We show the full cost of a loan — rate, processing fee, prepayment penalty — not just the headline number.
🆓
Always Free
Every tool, every comparison, every guide — free forever. We earn through advertising, not user fees.
🤝
Borrower First
Our goal is to help you make a better borrowing decision — even if that means recommending you wait and improve your CIBIL first.

💰 How We Keep the Lights On

LoanBuddy is funded by display advertising (Google AdSense). When you visit the site, you may see ads from Google — these are unrelated to our loan comparisons and do not influence which lenders we recommend or how we rank them.

In the future, we may introduce a DSA (Direct Selling Agent) model where we refer users to specific lenders and earn a referral fee. If and when we do this, we will clearly disclose it on each relevant page. Our comparisons will remain independent and unsponsored.

📅 How We Keep Data Current

Interest rates, processing fees, and eligibility criteria change frequently. We review and update all rate tables monthly. Each comparison page shows the last-updated month. Despite our best efforts, rates may change between our updates and your application — always verify directly with the lender.

Found a rate that's outdated or incorrect? Please let us know — we update within 48 hours of being notified.
