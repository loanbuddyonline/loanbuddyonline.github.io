🎯 How to Choose the Right Loan

1. Compare total cost, not just rate
Processing fee + GST + insurance can add 2–5% to your real cost. Always calculate total repayment amount, not just monthly EMI.
2. Check prepayment charges
Planning to pay off early? Banks like SBI charge nothing. Some NBFCs charge 4–6% on outstanding amount. This matters if you get a bonus.
3. Match tenure to your cash flow
Longer tenure = lower EMI but more interest. Shorter tenure = higher EMI but saves lakhs. Use our EMI calculator to find your sweet spot.
4. Apply to max 2 lenders at once
Each application is a hard inquiry on your CIBIL. Applying to 5 banks drops your score by 25–50 points. Short-list 2 and apply simultaneously.
5. Negotiate — always
If your CIBIL is 750+, always ask for 0.25–0.5% rate reduction and zero processing fee. Banks want your business — especially salaried customers.
6. Read the fine print on insurance
Banks often bundle loan protection insurance. It's optional — you can decline. Forced insurance on a ₹30L loan can cost ₹80,000–₹1.5L extra.

🏦 Bank vs NBFC — Which is Better?

🏦 Bank

✅Lower interest rates (8.5–15%)
✅Lower processing fees
✅More regulated — RBI governed
✅Better for large amounts (₹10L+)
❌Strict eligibility — 750+ CIBIL needed
❌Slow processing — 3–7 days
❌Lots of paperwork
Best for: Salaried with 750+ CIBIL needing ₹5L+ at the lowest rate

⚡ NBFC / App

✅Approves low CIBIL (even 550+)
✅Very fast — 10 minutes to 24 hours
✅Minimal documents — Aadhar + PAN
✅Accepts gig and self-employed income
❌Higher rates (17–45%)
❌Higher processing fees (2–6%)
❌Smaller loan amounts (up to ₹5L)
Best for: Gig workers, low salary, urgent need, low CIBIL borrowers
